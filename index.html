About Me

Hello! My name is Michael! I graduated from Georgia Southern University in 2017 with a Bachelor of Science in Geology and a minor in Geospatial Information Systems (GIS). After realizing I didn't want to be a geologist, I decided to pursue a more technical career. I currently work for Southern Company Gas as a GIS Specialist and realized very quickly that I want to learn how to code so I signed up for the Georgia Tech Coding Boot Camp. I hope, by taking this class, I will gain the skills necessary to advance my career whether I choose to stay in GIS or pursue something else.

Some interesting things to know about me are that I'm a long distance runner, I love to travel, I've been to Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Czech Republic and the Netherlands. I'm obsessed with Game of Thrones, I eat a lot of dark chocolate with almonds and sea salt and I can speak a little bit of German.
